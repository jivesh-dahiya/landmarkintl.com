About Our Hostel

Our hostel facilities are designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and nurturing environment for students. With modern amenities and a focus on community living, we ensure that every student feels at home.

Facilities Offered



Spacious and Well-Furnished Rooms


Our hostel offers a variety of room options to suit the needs and preferences of our students, including single, double, and shared accommodations. Each room is thoughtfully designed with ample space, comfortable beds, study desks, storage units, and good ventilation. Modern furnishings and natural lighting create a welcoming atmosphere, making it easy for students to focus on their studies and unwind after a busy day. Regular maintenance ensures that all rooms remain clean, safe, and in excellent condition throughout the year.

24/7 Security and Surveillance


The safety and security of our students are our top priorities. Our hostel is equipped with round-the-clock security personnel and advanced surveillance systems, including CCTV cameras in all common areas. Strict access control ensures that only authorized individuals can enter the premises, providing parents and students with peace of mind at all times.

Common Areas for Study


Dedicated study rooms and quiet zones are available for students who wish to focus on their academic work. These areas are well-lit, comfortable, and equipped with necessary resources to support group discussions, independent study, and collaborative projects.

Recreation Spaces


To promote relaxation and social interaction, our hostel features recreation rooms with indoor games, comfortable seating, and entertainment options. Outdoor spaces and sports facilities are also available, encouraging students to stay active and build friendships outside of their academic commitments.

Availablity of nutritious food prepared by chefs


At Landmark International School Hostel, we understand the importance of a balanced diet for growing minds and bodies. Our dining facilities offer a wide variety of wholesome, freshly prepared meals each day. Menus are carefully planned by experienced chefs and nutritionists to ensure students receive all essential nutrients. We accommodate diverse dietary preferences and needs, making sure every student enjoys delicious, healthy food that supports their academic and personal well-being.

Access to Laundry and Cleaning Services


We provide convenient laundry and cleaning services to ensure students have a hassle-free living experience.

Our hostel offers on-site laundry facilities, allowing students to easily manage their personal belongings and maintain hygiene. Regular cleaning of rooms and common areas is carried out by our dedicated housekeeping staff, ensuring a clean, comfortable, and healthy environment for everyone. These services help students focus on their studies and extracurricular activities without worrying about daily chores.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our hostel facilities, feel free to reach out via our Contact Page. We are here to assist you!
